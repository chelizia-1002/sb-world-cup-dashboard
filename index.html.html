⚽ How points work

Office hype points: Group win = 3, draw = 1, team reaches knockouts = 5 bonus, reaches Round of 16 = 8 bonus, quarter-final = 12 bonus, semi-final = 18 bonus, final = 25 bonus, champion = 40 bonus.
